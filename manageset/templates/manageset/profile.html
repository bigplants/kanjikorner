  1. Welcome!

    Kanjisama is an SRS (spaced repition system) application that can help you learn 2000 kanji and thousands of the most common Japanese words. We help you rapidly build your vocab based on kanji you already know and give you the tools to learn new kanji at your own pace.

  2. Creating Lessons

    Create lessons using words you want to learn from a list of the 10,000 most common words organized by the kanji you already know.

  3. Reviewing Lessons

    After you create a lesson, come back here to review your lesson. When you review a lesson you'll go through all the words one time with the reading and definition shown, and then one more time without the that information. Once you complete a lesson all those words will be added to your review queue .

  4. The Review Queue

    This is your review queue. Once you complete a lesson the words will be added to it. Kanjisama will automatically tell you when it's time to review your words. When words are ready to review they will appear here (just click!), so make sure to check back often!

  5. Progress Chart

    This area lets you visually keep track of your words through our SRS system. Once you complete a lesson, words will move from the "零" pile to the "一"　pile. Then as you review words from your review queue, words will either go up a level or down a level depending on if you successfully remembered the reading and meaning of the review word. As a word moves up each level, the time until you see the word will keep increasing. This way you will practice words you don't know well more frequently and words you know less frequently. When a word gets to the end it will no longer show up!

  6. Let's get started

    This shows how many kanji you know! Right now we need to figure how many you already know (it's okay if you don't know any!). Click this circle to get started!
