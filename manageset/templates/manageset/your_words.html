  1. Create Stacks Page

    This is where you can create lessons for words you want to learn.

    1. Add words to your lesson.
    2. Remove words you don't want to see in the future
    3. Click create lesson to create it
    4. You'll be directed to the homepage where you can review the lesson

  2. If you know it

    Click the "Add" button to add a word to your lesson.

    Hint - We suggest that you don't put more than 20 words in a lesson to start off until you get a feel for things.

  3. If you already know it...

    ...or don't want to see it again just click the X and it will disappear next time you visit the page.

  4. Your Words tab

    You are currently in this area. When you are here you will be shown words that only contain kanji that you have already added.

  5. Kanji Filter

    User this to filter by individual or multiple kanji. This can be really useful if you just recently added a new kanji and want to find words with that kanji.

  6. All Words tab

    This tab allows you to search all words by kanji, definition, or hiragana readings.

  7. Create your first stack

    Once you've added the words you want to learn, click this button to create the lesson! This is will take you back to the main page where you can then see your stack and practice it!

  8. Filter words by kanji

    If you want to see the words available to you for only specific kanji, go to this tab and click the checkmark for the kanji you want to filter by. Hint - this can be really helpful when you add new kanji you want to learn!
